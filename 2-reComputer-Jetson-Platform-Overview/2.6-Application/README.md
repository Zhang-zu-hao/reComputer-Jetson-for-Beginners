# Application Scenarios for reComputer Jetson

[Back to Module 2](../README.md) | [Back to Table of Contents](../../Table-of-Contents.md)

reComputer Jetson devices are designed for edge AI workloads that need GPU acceleration, compact deployment, and low-power operation. This section serves as a quick bridge between the platform overview and the hands-on modules that follow in this course.

## Typical Application Areas

- Computer vision, including detection, tracking, segmentation, and smart video analytics
- Robotics, including perception, navigation, and multi-sensor control
- Generative AI at the edge, including local chatbots and multimodal assistants
- Industrial and retail systems, including monitoring, quality inspection, and automation
- Education and prototyping, including AI experiments, embedded Linux learning, and accelerated development
